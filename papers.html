What follows is a list of papers that mention Freesound or use Freesound data for the research. This list is created automatically by finding articles that cite one of the two main Freesound reference papers. Some entries have also been added manually. Papers are sorted by year of publication and alphabetically by first author surname.

If you have a paper which should be on the list and is not, please let us know at freesound@freesound.org.
