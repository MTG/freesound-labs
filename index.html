Welcome to Freesound Labs!

Freesound Labs is a directory of projects, hacks, apps, research and other initiatives that use content from Freesound or use the Freesound API. You can use the list below to explore what has been done using Freesound, or use the Browse section to search content according to tags and categories. Check the About page for more information on Freesound Labs. This site is run by the Freesound Team at the Music Technology Group of Universitat Pompeu Fabra. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions, comments or suggestions.
